# Fitbit Health Sync

Fitbit Health Sync helps users auto-sync Fitbit data to Apple Health.

## What it does

- Connects to your Fitbit account using OAuth.
- Reads selected Fitbit health metrics.
- Writes those metrics to Apple Health.
- Supports periodic best-effort background syncing on iPhone.
