# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-02-21_

Fitbit Health Sync respects your privacy.

## Data We Access

With your explicit authorization, the app may access selected Fitbit data such as:

- Body weight
- Body fat percentage
- Steps
- Sleep
- Resting heart rate
- Active energy

## How Data Is Used

- Data is read from Fitbit and written to Apple Health to keep your records in sync.
- Access tokens are stored securely on your device (Keychain).

## Data Storage

- The app is designed to process data on-device.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## Third-Party Services

- Fitbit API is used for data access based on your authorization.
- Apple HealthKit is used to write data into Apple Health.

## Your Choices

- You can disconnect Fitbit at any time in the app.
- You can revoke Fitbit app access in your Fitbit account settings.
- You can uninstall the app at any time.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.
